About the Event

More Than a Tournament.
A Final Festival.

The EON Interprogram Finalists Cup is the ultimate farewell for the Class of 2026 — an entire day where the pitch, the music, the colors, and the people all come together for one last shared memory.

"

You spent years building something here — friendships, knowledge, late nights, and big dreams. Now, before you go, you celebrate one last time together.

— EON Interprogram Finalists Cup 2026

This isn't just a football tournament. It's a full-day farewell festival exclusively for every final-year student across all 25 programs at Mbarara University of Science & Technology. A day of competition, dancing, aerobics, colors, music, cake, and the kind of joy that only comes at the end of something truly great.

On April 19th, MUST Town Campus Pitch becomes the last shared ground of the Class of 2026. The tournament is exclusively for finalists — this is YOUR moment. Continuing students are warmly welcome to come as supporters. Come in your brightest colors. Leave as legends.
