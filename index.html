About the Event

More Than a Tournament.
A Final Chapter.

The EON Interprogram Finalists Cup is the ultimate farewell for the Class of 2026 — a day where the pitch becomes the stage for one last shared memory before you leave Mbarara University forever.

"

You spent years building something here — friendships, knowledge, late nights, and big dreams. Now, before you go, you play one last game together.

— EON Interprogram Finalists Cup 2026

This isn't just a football tournament. It's a send-off celebration for every final-year student across all 25 programs at Mbarara University of Science & Technology. A day of competition, reunion, music, food, and the kind of joy that only comes at the end of something great.

On April 19th, Mbarara Sports Arena becomes the last shared ground of the Class of 2026. Come in your brightest colors. Leave as legends.

01 — Event
🎓
Finalists' Farewell
A fitting send-off for graduating students across all programs. Celebrate your final year in the most legendary way possible — on the turf, with your people, one last time.
02 — Community
🤝
Interprogram Unity
25 programs, one arena. Build bridges across faculties, make lifelong memories, and share the pitch with faces from across the campus you may have never met.
03 — Competition
⚽
7-a-Side Tournament
Fast-paced, high-energy matches on premium artificial turf. Group stages + knockout rounds — all decided in one glorious day.
04 — Inclusivity
🌟
Mixed Squads Welcome
Can't fill a squad with finalists alone? Continuing students from the same program can join — because no program gets left out of the farewell.
05 — Festival
🎉
A Full-Day Festival
Music, board games, chess, ice cream, snacks, and drinks all day long. The send-off party you always imagined your final year deserved.
06 — Style

Show up in your most vibrant, eye-catching outfit. Let's make Mbarara Sports Arena a sea of color, energy, and pure joy for the Class of 2026.

The Venue

Mbarara Sports Arena

A world-class sports complex with a premium artificial turf pitch, volleyball court, netball pitch, and swimming pool. The perfect stage for the Class of 2026's final act.

  • Premium artificial turf soccer pitch — 7-a-side ready
  • Full-size volleyball court for sideline fun
  • Netball pitch for champions off the ball
  • Mini swimming pool — cool off between matches
  • Dedicated spectator areas with full views
  • Food & drinks zone, board game corner, music stage
  • Gorgeous venue. You just have to be there. 🔥

Secure Your Spot

Assemble Your Squad
Gather up to 14 players from your program. Finalists have priority, but continuing students from the same program can fill remaining spots.
02
Contact the Organizers
Reach out via the numbers opposite to express your program's interest and get full registration details.
03
Pay the Registration Fee
Register your team by paying UGX 130,000. Payment details will be confirmed by the organizers on contact.
04
Submit Player List
Provide the names of all registered players (max 14). All players must be enrolled in your program.
