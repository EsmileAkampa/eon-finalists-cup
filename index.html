// About the Event

More Than a Tournament

The EON Interprogram Finalists Cup is a celebration of achievement, unity, and the bonds forged across programs. As finalists take their final bow, they do it on the pitch.

🎓
Finalists Farewell
A fitting send-off for graduating students across all programs. Celebrate your final year in the most legendary way possible — on the turf, with your people.
🤝
Interprogram Unity
25 programs, one arena. Build bridges across faculties, make lifelong memories, and discover faces you've never met across the campus.
⚽
7-a-Side Format
Fast-paced, high-energy matches on Mbarara Sports Arena's premium turf pitch. Group stages + knockouts all in one epic day.
🌟
Mixed Squads Allowed
Can't fill a squad with finalists alone? Continuing students from the same program can join — because no program gets left out.
🎉
Festival Atmosphere
Music, board games, chess, ice cream, snacks, and drinks all day long. This isn't just a tournament — it's a full-day festival.
👗
Dress Code: Bright Colors
Show up in your most vibrant, eye-catching outfit. Let's make Mbarara Sports Arena a sea of color, energy, and joy.
👗 Dress Code — Any Bright Color

Secure Your Spot

25 programs. Limited squad spots. Don't let your program miss out on the biggest farewell party of 2026.

01
Assemble Your Squad
Gather up to 14 players from your program. Finalists have priority, but continuing students of the same program can fill remaining spots.
02
Contact the Organizers
Reach out via the numbers below to express your program's interest and receive registration instructions.
03
Pay the Registration Fee
Register your team by paying UGX 130,000. Payment details will be provided by the organizers upon contact.
04
Submit Player List
Provide names and details of all registered players (max 14). All players must be enrolled in your program.
05
Show Up & Shine
Arrive by 7:00 AM on April 19th in your brightest colors. The turf awaits. History will be written.
